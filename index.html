Our Story — Personal Experience

Founder: Penélope Farías — Chef & community advocate

I created NutriGo Kitchen after years of juggling family life and work in a small town with limited healthy takeout. I wanted meals that respected dietary needs, tasted great, and were simple to prepare. That became a mission: make nutritious eating effortless for people who are short on time but long on standards.

“NutriGo has changed our evenings. The kids eat better, and I spend less time cooking. The meals are fresh, flavorful, and actually filling.”

— Isabel R., working mother of two
